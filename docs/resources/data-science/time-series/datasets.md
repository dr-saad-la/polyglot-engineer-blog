# Time Series Datasets

## Competition Datasets

1. **M5 Forecasting - Accuracy** [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/c/m5-forecasting-accuracy/data)
   Hierarchical sales forecasting competition.

2. **Store Item Demand Forecasting** [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/c/demand-forecasting-kernels-only/data) 5 years of sales data, 50 items, 10 stores.

3. **Web Traffic Time Series Forecasting**[Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/c/web-traffic-time-series-forecasting) Wikipedia page views. Large-scale forecasting.

## Benchmark Collections

1. **Monash Time Series Forecasting Archive** [Website](https://forecastingdata.org/)
   Comprehensive collection of forecasting datasets.

2. **M Competitions** [GitHub](https://github.com/Mcompetitions) M1, M3, M4, M5 - standard
   forecasting benchmarks.

3. **UCI Machine Learning Repository** [Website](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets.php?format=&task=&att=&area=&numAtt=&numIns=&type=ts) Various time series datasets for classification and forecasting.

## Domain-Specific

### Financial Data
- **Yahoo Finance API:** `yfinance` Python package
- **Alpha Vantage:** Free stock API
- **Quandl:** Financial and economic data

### Energy & Weather
- **NREL:** Solar and wind data
- **NOAA:** Climate data
- **Open Power System Data:** European energy data

### IoT & Sensors
- **NASA:** Spacecraft telemetry
- **PhysioNet:** Medical time series
- **CRAWDAD:** Wireless sensor data
