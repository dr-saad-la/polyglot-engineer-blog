# Important Time Series Papers

## Classical Methods

1. **Box & Jenkins (1970)**: "Time Series Analysis: Forecasting and Control" Foundation of ARIMA
   methodology.

2. **Holt-Winters**: "Forecasting Seasonals and Trends by Exponentially Weighted Moving
   Averages" Exponential smoothing methods.

## Modern ML

1. **N-BEATS (2020)** [arXiv:1905.10437](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.10437) "Neural basis
   expansion analysis for interpretable time series forecasting"

2. **Temporal Fusion Transformers (2021)**: [arXiv:1912.09363](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.09363)
   "Temporal Fusion Transformers for Interpretable Multi-horizon Time Series Forecasting"

3. **Informer (2021)**: [arXiv:2012.07436](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.07436) "Beyond Efficient
   Transformer for Long Sequence Time-Series Forecasting"

4. **DeepAR (2020)**: Amazon's probabilistic forecasting with autoregressive recurrent networks.
